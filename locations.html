FH | Mandela Bay

Pastor George Georgiou

Pastor George started out his ministry as a student at Nelson Mandela Metropolitan University
and a member of the AOG in 1991. He birthed a student community by starting a small group
in his residence which grew into a student church under Every Nation Africa.

After 12 years of fruitful ministry, Pastor George planted Father’s House Church in 2010
and held its first service on Christmas Day with around 200 people attending. Since then,
exponential growth has seen Father’s House boldly moving into a warehouse in North End,
Port Elizabeth, now with 3000 people in Sunday attendance and over 5000 people who call
Father’s House home.
